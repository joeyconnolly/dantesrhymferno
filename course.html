The Cartography of Verse: Course Overview

Welcome to "The Depths of Poetry," an immersive exploration of language's transformative potential. This twelve-week journey traverses the liminal spaces where craft meets consciousness, where technical precision serves as the foundation for profound expression. Through careful examination of seminal works and rigorous creative practice, we will map the complex intersections between form and meaning, tradition and innovation, personal voice and universal resonance.

Poetry is not only dream and vision; it is the skeleton architecture of our lives. It lays the foundations for a future of change, a bridge across our fears of what has never been before.

— Audre Lorde

This course eschews simplistic approaches to poetry, recognizing that true mastery emerges from the dialectical relationship between intuitive expression and disciplined attention. Each session builds upon previous explorations, creating a developmental arc that mirrors the recursive nature of artistic growth itself—where each new insight necessitates the revisiting and deepening of fundamental principles.

Hermeneutic Immersion

Engage with carefully curated poems that serve as nexus points for understanding diverse poetic philosophies and methodologies. Each reading functions simultaneously as aesthetic experience and analytical object, revealing the complex interplay between technique and vision.

Praxis & Experimentation

Transform theoretical understanding into embodied knowledge through structured creative exercises that challenge conventional approaches to composition. These practical laboratories invite participants to inhabit unfamiliar linguistic territories while developing a more nuanced relationship with their own creative processes.

Dialectical Engagement

Participate in substantive discourse that transcends superficial appreciation to examine the philosophical, cultural, and psychological dimensions of poetic expression. These communal explorations foster an environment where individual insights contribute to collective understanding.

Transformative Objectives

Through sustained engagement with this curriculum, participants will:

  • 01 Develop a sophisticated understanding of poetry's formal elements and their relationship to meaning-making
  • 02 Cultivate analytical frameworks that illuminate the multidimensional nature of poetic texts
  • 03 Expand their creative repertoire through deliberate experimentation with diverse techniques and traditions
  • 04 Establish reflective practices that integrate critical feedback within an evolving artistic consciousness
  • 05 Foster connections within a community of practitioners committed to the advancement of poetic craft

Pedagogical Philosophy

Our approach to poetic education rests on the premise that understanding emerges through multiple modes of engagement—intellectual, creative, and dialogic. The course integrates these complementary dimensions to create a holistic learning ecology.

Directed Reading

Carefully selected poems that exemplify particular techniques, traditions, or philosophical approaches. Each reading is contextualized within broader literary and cultural frameworks.

Writing Exercises

Structured creative opportunities that challenge participants to apply theoretical concepts in concrete ways, expanding technical versatility while developing individual voice.

Discussion Prompts

Thoughtfully crafted questions that stimulate analytical thinking and facilitate substantive dialogue about poetic texts, traditions, and philosophical underpinnings.

Interactive Sessions

Collaborative virtual gatherings where participants share insights, receive feedback, and engage with the community of fellow practitioners in real-time exchange.

Reflective Practice

Ongoing documentation of creative development, fostering metacognitive awareness and helping participants trace their evolving relationship with poetic craft.
